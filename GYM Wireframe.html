PRIORITY · Workout vs Lock indicator (what happens when both are active)

They never compete: an active workout always takes the floating slot — starting a workout is exactly what the lock wants. The lock indicator yields to a slim "locked until you finish" strip; the system Live Activity / Dynamic Island shows the workout timer. Finishing the workout lifts the lock entirely. Abandoning it brings the lock indicator back.
