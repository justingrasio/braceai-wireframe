ACTIVE · Shield, Live Activity, unlock by workout

On a training day, opening a locked app shows the branded shield. The shield can't open our app (Apple rule), so we keep it to a single Close button and put the actions on the Live Activity — Start workout, Mini, or a break. Finishing the workout unlocks everything and the Live Activity disappears (no separate "unlocked" screen).

PRIORITY · Workout vs Lock indicator (what happens when both are active)

The lock indicator yields to a slim "locked until you finish" strip; the system Live Activity / Dynamic Island shows the workout timer. Finishing the workout lifts the lock entirely. Abandoning it brings the lock indicator back.
